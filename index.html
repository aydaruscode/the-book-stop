The Book Stop

A small book rental by Aishah for her friends and schoolmates. Pick a book, book your dates, enjoy reading, and return it when you're done.

Aishah started The Book Stop because she loves reading and hopes to encourage more children to enjoy books too. Since books are not always cheap, she wanted to make it easier for her friends to borrow and enjoy different stories at a small and affordable cost — while also learning to care for books responsibly.

Library Rules

Please read these before borrowing, so the books can stay clean and ready for the next reader.

  • Please take care of the book properly.
  • Do not tear, fold, wet, or write on the pages.
  • Please return the book on the agreed date.
  • Payment is by cash when the book is received at school.
  • Please care for the books like they are your own so more readers can continue enjoying them too.
